Team Lead: Jagrut Brahmbhatt — Professor: Dr. Suresh Sitaraman

As flip-chip packaging moves toward glass core substrates, it introduces new challenges in mechanical reliability, particularly under thermomechanical stress from cyclic temperature variations. Our goal is to evaluate solder joint fatigue and predict failure using advanced simulation methods.

  • 2D diagonal cross-section modeled in ANSYS using PLANE183 elements
  • Shared topology meshing to ensure stress continuity
  • Anand viscoplastic model applied to SnAg solder material
  • Other materials modeled with bilinear and temp-dependent elasticity

Fatigue life cycles predicted using two empirical models:

  • Kanchanomai Model
  • Andersson Model

The lower cycle count was considered for conservative reliability assessment.
